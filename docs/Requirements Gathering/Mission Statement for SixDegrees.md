# **Mission Statement for SixDegrees:**
To build an open-source, decentralized professional ecosystem anchored in verified trust, user ownership, and radical transparency—transitioning the digital workforce from "Surveillance Sociality" to "Sovereign Utility," and creating an infrastructure designed for the dignity and prosperity of the human professional 1-3.
To fulfill this mission, SixDegrees operates on four foundational pillars that fundamentally reconstruct the social contract between the professional and the platform:
**1\. Reclaiming Authenticity Through Verified Truth**We are done pretending that performative hype, engagement-bait, and unverified claims serve the modern workforce 4-6. SixDegrees is a bot-free, entirely human ecosystem where identity and credentials are mathematically proven, not self-reported 4, 7. By utilizing cryptographically signed Verifiable Credentials and Zero-Trust Authentication, we ensure that every participant is real and every achievement is backed by transparent Proof of Work 8, 9. We prioritize demonstrated knowledge and peer-validated merit over AI-generated "thought leadership" 4, 10.
**2\. Establishing Absolute Sovereign Ownership**You are not the product, and your professional identity should not be held hostage by a centralized corporate database 11, 12. SixDegrees shifts power directly back to the individual through Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) and open protocols 12, 13. We guarantee absolute data portability and platform sovereignty—meaning your network, content, and reputation belong exclusively to you, insulated from algorithmic manipulation, third-party tracking, and data lock-in 11, 12, 14.
**3\. Engineering Merit-Driven, Bias-Free Opportunity**The recruitment process should not be a "black hole" of ghosting, fake jobs, and information asymmetry 15, 16. We are replacing the legacy hiring market with a transparent, accountable pipeline that mandates salary visibility and tracks employer responsiveness 15, 17. By leveraging Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) and secure enclaves, talent discovery becomes completely blind to Personal Identifiable Information (PII) 18, 19. We ensure that the hiring funnel is driven entirely by attested skills and merit, structurally preventing unconscious bias and evaluating candidates strictly on what they can do, not who they are or where they come from 20-22.
**4\. Respecting Cognitive Bandwidth and Human Attention**We fundamentally reject the surveillance economy, persuasive design, and addictive "infinite scroll" mechanics engineered to extract your time 11, 18, 23. SixDegrees is designed for cognitive ergonomics, built to integrate seamlessly with your deep work schedule 23, 24. Through linear interfaces, suppressed public metrics, and value-aligned curation, we operate as a high-signal tool that acts as an extension of the professional mind, rather than a distraction from it 1, 23, 25.
